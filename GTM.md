# GTM.md — SpendLens

## Exact Target User
**Engineering Manager (EM) or CTO** at a Series A/B startup (20-100 employees).
- **Pain point:** AI tool costs have ballooned from $500/mo to $5,000+/mo with no oversight.
- **Trigger:** Monthly finance review showing a spike in "SaaS & Software" line items.

## Acquisition Channels ($0 Budget)

### 1. Community Outreach (High Intent)
- **Show HN:** Post a "Show HN" on Hacker News with a focus on the deterministic engine and privacy (no login).
- **Reddit:** Engage in `r/startups`, `r/SaaS`, and `r/ExperiencedDevs`. Share a "State of AI Spend 2026" post using anonymized data from the first 50 audits.
- **Slack/Discord:** Post in Lenny's Slack (#tools channel), YC Alumni Slack, and specialized EM communities.

### 2. Viral Loop (Built-in)
- **Shareable Results:** Every audit generates a unique URL. When an EM finds $2,000/mo in savings, they share the link with their CFO or other EMs in their network.

### 3. Content Marketing (SEO)
- **Tool Comparison:** Write "Claude Team vs. Claude Pro: When to switch?" or "Is Cursor Pro redundant if I have Copilot?"
- **Target Keywords:** "reduce AI tool costs", "startup AI budget optimization", "Cursor vs Copilot cost comparison".

## First 100 Users Plan (30 Days)
- **Day 1-7:** Manual outreach to 50 CTOs on LinkedIn/Twitter. Offer a "White Glove Audit" where we run the tool for them.
- **Day 8-14:** Official "Show HN" launch.
- **Day 15-30:** Publish first data report on AI spend trends to drive second-wave traffic.

**Credex Customer Base:** Credex can distribute this tool to their existing database of 10,000+ developers and founders as a "Free Value-Add."
- Conversion: Audit -> High Savings -> Credex Discounted Credits pitch.
