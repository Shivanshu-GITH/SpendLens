# ECONOMICS.md — SpendLens

## Unit Economics

### 1. Cost per Audit (CPAu)
- **Anthropic API (Summary):** ~$0.002 (Claude 3 Haiku)
- **Supabase/Vercel:** $0 (Free tier for MVP)
- **Total Variable Cost:** **~$0.002 per audit**

### 2. Lead Value (LTV)
- **High-Savings Lead:** Defined as an audit identifying >$500/mo ($6,000/yr) in savings.
- **Conversion to Credex:** Estimate 5% of high-savings leads purchase a Credex credit bundle.
- **Avg. Credex Bundle Profit:** $500 - $2,000.
- **Blended Lead Value:** (0.05 * $1,250) = **$62.50 per high-savings lead.**

### 3. CAC & Payback
- **Organic CAC:** $0 (Time-based).
- **Paid CAC (Future):** If we pay $2.00 per click and have a 10% audit completion rate, CAC per audit = $20.00.
- If 20% of audits are "High-Savings," CAC per high-savings lead = $100.00.
- **Profitability:** $62.50 (LTV) vs $100.00 (CAC) indicates we need organic growth or higher conversion rates to scale via paid ads.

## Path to $1M ARR
To generate $1M in profit for Credex:
- Needs 800 Credex customers per year (~67/month).
- At 5% conversion, needs 1,340 high-savings leads/month.
- At 20% high-savings rate, needs **6,700 total audits per month.**

## Efficiency Metrics
- **Viral Coefficient:** Goal > 0.2 (1 in 5 users shares their result).
- **Completion Rate:** Goal > 50% (Landing page visit -> Audit result).
